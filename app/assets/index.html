EU Startup Hubs

EU StartupHubs is an ambitious two-year project to map Europe's startup ecosystem. The project is a key component of the European Commission's "Startup Europe" programme. On-the-ground research is being conducted by Grant Thornton. A sophisticated analytic platform is being developed by Trampoline Systems.

The beta release provides analysis on 10 hub cities across Europe using data sourced from AngelList and Crunchbase. The full release will extend this to 15 cities and add corporate registry data including revenue and employment intelligence where available. The cities currently covered are Berlin, Brussels, Bucherest, Helsinki, London, Madrid, Manchester, Munich, Paris and Stockholm.

If you're a startup in one of the featured cities and you'd like to be included on the map please email us with your company name, registered number, office address, website, twitter details, AngelList profile page and Crunchbase profile page. After the beta we'll be adding a form on the website.

Contact us at hello@startuphubs.eu
